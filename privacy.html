Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BHUKK. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our liquor delivery app and website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identification details (name, email, phone number)
  • Age verification data (government ID proof for legal compliance)
  • Payment and transaction information
  • Location and order delivery address
  • Usage data and analytics (to improve our service)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, verify age, improve customer experience, and comply with local liquor regulations. We never sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may include third-party payment gateways and delivery partners who have their own privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. To make such requests, contact us at support@bhukk.com.

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but some site features may not function properly as a result.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us at privacy@bhukk.com.
